Description

A website (previously known as Awesome Schedule) which helps UVa students to schedule their classes more efficiently. Get your class selection done with the searching field and customize them with filters and sort options. Once you've finalized your class selection, hit "Generate" button and get all the possible schedules satisfying to your requirements.
